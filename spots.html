Best Spots In NYC

In my opinion

One World Trade Center

Went here once, going on the elevator up made my ears pop but also showed a very scenic view of NYC

Coney Island

I don't really like the rides at Luna Park but the nice cool beach breeze is good

The Oculus

Looks like a rib cage or some pokemon

Worst Spots in NYC

Subway

Always crowded in the morning. For some reason has delays and rerouts in the worst possible times.

Any Public Bathroom

Public bathrooms are dirty and toliet paper is almost always littered around the floor.
